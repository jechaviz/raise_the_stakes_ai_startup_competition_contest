# Risks

## Risk Register

| Risk | Level | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| Deadline conflict between June 10 and July 1 copy | high | Treat June 10 as hard deadline; submit as early as possible. |
| Dealum requires login and JS | high | Prepare payload and form map; use WAIBAv visible session for authenticated draft/final submit. |
| Legal entity details unavailable in repo | high | Keep secure applicant inputs out of git; fill directly in Dealum/private evidence. |
| Two-person team proof missing | high | Add founder/member details before final submit. |
| Funding cap ambiguity EUR vs USD | medium | Use official EUR 10M cap as primary; ensure total raised is below both thresholds if possible. |
| Traction evidence too thin | medium | Collect five user conversations and two written validations before final submission. |
| Product perceived as services rather than software | medium | Demo reusable V generator, web workspace, field maps, and automation receipts. |
| AI core perceived as shallow | medium | Emphasize extraction, scoring, critique, evidence-gap detection, and agentic automation. |
| External form changes | medium | Re-run WAIBAv form inspection before final submit and update `dealum_form_map.yml`. |
| Privacy leakage | medium | Public docs contain summaries only; customer/legal/funding proof stays private. |

## Submission Safety

- Do not commit credentials, legal IDs, cap table, passports, or customer PII.
- Final submit requires an authenticated Dealum session and final package flag.
- Capture confirmation receipt after submit.
- Preserve public/private evidence separation.
