# Eligibility Checklist

Source snapshot: 2026-05-29.

Primary source: https://www.raisesummit.com/startup-competition

Partner listing: https://vc4a.com/raise/raise-summit-2026-startup-competition/

## Competition Eligibility

- [ ] AI is central to the product.
  - Evidence: `docs/PRODUCT_AI_PROPOSED.md`
- [ ] Startup has raised no more than EUR 10M total funding.
  - Note: VC4A copy says "$10M"; official RAISE page says EUR 10M.
- [ ] Startup is a registered legal entity.
  - Evidence: legal name, registration country, entity type, registration ID.
- [ ] Team has at least two members or two dedicated cofounders.
  - Evidence: founder names, roles, LinkedIn/profile URLs, commitment.
- [ ] Startup is early stage: pre-seed, seed, or Series A.
  - Evidence: funding stage and round summary.
- [ ] Product has a live product or strong MVP.
  - Evidence: demo URL, local demo panel, generated evidence package.
- [ ] Product has early traction or strong validation.
  - Evidence: `docs/TRACTION_PROOF_MINIMUM.md`
- [ ] At least one founder can attend finals in Paris if selected.
  - Finals: July 8, 2026 and July 9, 2026.

## Application Readiness

- [ ] Dealum account exists or can be created.
- [ ] Company profile in Dealum can be shared with RAISE.
- [ ] Website URL is ready.
- [ ] Demo URL is ready.
- [ ] Deck URL or PDF is ready.
- [ ] Video URL is ready if requested by Dealum.
- [ ] Short description, long description, market, traction, and team answers
  are ready in `docs/APPLICATION_PACKET.md`.
- [ ] Funding raised, stage, and legal entity fields are accurate.
- [ ] Founder contact email and phone are accurate.
- [ ] Data room paths do not expose secrets in public repos.

## Secure Applicant Inputs

These values belong in Dealum or private evidence storage, not in public docs:

- Registered legal entity name.
- Registration number or equivalent.
- Incorporation country and date.
- Founder legal names, contact email, and phone.
- Cap table and funding raised.
- Investor names if not public.
- Revenue, bank, invoice, or customer-identifying evidence.
- Passport, travel, or visa details for Paris attendance.
