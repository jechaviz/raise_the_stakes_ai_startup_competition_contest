# Traction Proof Minimum

Goal: assemble enough proof for an early-stage AI startup application even
before broad public launch.

## Minimum Application Proof

- [ ] Live or local demo panel with RAISE scenario.
- [ ] Three opportunity packages generated:
  - Build with Gemini XPRIZE.
  - RAISE the STAKES.
  - One more `ai_startup_pitch` package.
- [ ] Five target-user conversations with founders, grant writers, operators,
  or accelerators.
- [ ] Two written validations:
  - testimonial,
  - LOI,
  - pilot request,
  - or paid sprint intent.
- [ ] One repeatable automation receipt from WAIBAv.
- [ ] One evidence ledger showing public/private proof boundaries.
- [ ] One clear business model quote:
  - paid sprint,
  - monthly workspace,
  - or accelerator/team package.

## Outreach Script

Subject: Quick validation for an AI application-ops tool

Message:

I am validating ContestOps AI, a tool that turns startup competitions, grants,
and accelerator applications into executable checklists, evidence ledgers, pitch
assets, and form automation. Could I run one opportunity through it for you and
ask for 10 minutes of feedback?

## Testimonial Prompt

- What opportunity/application workflow did we process?
- What manual work did this reduce?
- Would you use or pay for this in a real deadline sprint?
- May we quote your feedback in a startup competition application?
