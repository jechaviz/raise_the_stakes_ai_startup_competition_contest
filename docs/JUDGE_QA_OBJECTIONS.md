# Judge QA Objections

Prepared: 2026-05-29

Goal: answer predictable RAISE judge objections with crisp, evidence-backed
responses. Keep answers under 30 seconds unless the judge asks for depth.

## Default Opening Answer

ContestOps AI is an application-ops agent for founders. It turns startup
competitions, accelerators, grants, hackathons, and RFPs into execution plans,
evidence ledgers, pitch assets, structured payloads, form automation, and
submission receipts. The RAISE application package is the live demo.

## Red-Team Follow-Ups

### "Show me something that is not a document."

Answer:

The documents are the audit trail. The non-document product layer is the
generator and automation flow: a source becomes structured artifacts, field
maps, and a browser prep playbook that can be rerun and receipt-backed.

### "Why will this not become a services agency?"

Answer:

Services are the wedge only because founders buy outcomes under deadlines. The
product surface is repeatable: opportunity schema, scoring, artifact generator,
evidence ledger, form maps, QA gates, and receipts.

### "Where can this fail?"

Answer:

It can fail if the team cannot collect validation quickly, if portal automation
is unreliable without human review, or if the product is positioned as writing
instead of operations. The current sprint plan directly targets those risks.
