# Product AI Proposed

Product name: `ContestOps AI`

One-liner: AI-native operations for founders turning high-value opportunities
into shipped products, evidence packs, and completed submissions.

## Problem

Founders lose time and money because startup competitions, grants, hackathons,
and procurement opportunities require many brittle steps: finding fit, reading
rules, building an MVP, preparing evidence, writing the pitch, filling external
forms, tracking deadlines, and proving traction. Most teams handle this through
manual documents and scattered browser sessions.

## Solution

ContestOps AI converts an opportunity URL or local brief into an executable
application workspace:

- extracts rules, deadlines, field requirements, and judging criteria;
- generates checklists, evidence ledgers, submission drafts, and runbooks;
- creates demo and pitch assets;
- runs external form preparation through WAIBAv automation;
- tracks production readiness and missing proof.

## Why AI Is Core

The product is not a generic dashboard with a chatbot. AI is embedded in the
workflow:

- opportunity interpretation and requirement extraction;
- scoring of fit, effort, deadline risk, and ROI;
- generation of tailored pitch/application answers;
- critique loops against judging criteria;
- evidence-gap detection;
- agentic browser/form execution with receipts.

- generated V manifest;
- structured Dealum payload;
- application answers;
- landing/deck/video outline;
- traction proof minimum;
- automation plan and playbooks;

## Business Model

- Paid application sprints for founders and small teams.
- Recurring opportunity operations workspace.
- Success-based upgrade where the program rules and jurisdiction allow it.
- Team subscriptions for accelerators, studios, and professional services.

## RAISE Fit

RAISE looks for early-stage AI companies with a live product or strong MVP,
early traction, clear market path, strong team, and distinctive product. This
package positions ContestOps AI as applied AI infrastructure for founders: a
workflow product that helps more startups find, launch, prove, and submit.
