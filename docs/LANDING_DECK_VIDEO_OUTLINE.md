   fragmented and evidence-heavy.
3. Customer: founders, startup studios, accelerators, grant teams, and services

4. Product: AI workspace that ingests rules, builds the package, and executes

   agentic form automation.
6. Demo: RAISE packet generated from source pages into checklist, evidence,
   payload, and automation.
7. Market: startup applications, grants, hackathons, RFPs, accelerators, and
   founder operations.
8. Business model: paid sprints, SaaS workspaces, accelerator/team plans.
9. Traction: processed opportunities, pilot users, LOIs, waitlist, receipts.
10. Team: two dedicated builders/operators with AI and automation capability.
11. Roadmap: ingestion depth, CRM/outreach, deploy receipts, Dealum/Devpost
    templates, production form execution.
12. Ask: RAISE exposure, investor intros, credits, and partner channels.
