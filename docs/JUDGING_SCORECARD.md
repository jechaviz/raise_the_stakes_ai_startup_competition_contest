# Judging Scorecard

Purpose: keep every asset pointed at the four RAISE signals: team, distinctive
product, traction or validation, and opportunity scale.

## Score Targets

| Criterion | Target Score | Current State | Proof Path |
|---|---:|---|---|
| Team strength | 8/10 | Secure founder inputs pending | Add two founder profiles, roles, AI/build evidence, and Paris availability. |
| Product distinctiveness | 9/10 | Strong | Show end-to-end application ops, not just writing or discovery. |
| Traction or validation | 7/10 | Collecting | Complete five conversations, two written validations, one pilot or LOI. |
| Scale of opportunity | 9/10 | Strong | Frame market as all founder opportunity operations, not one contest. |
| AI depth | 9/10 | Strong | Demonstrate extraction, scoring, critique, evidence-gap detection, and automation planning. |
| Application completeness | 9/10 | Strong | Dealum payload, field map, evidence ledger, and playbooks are ready. |
