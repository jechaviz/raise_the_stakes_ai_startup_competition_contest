# Application Answers

Use these answers as the source of truth for Dealum fields.

## Company

ContestOps AI.

## Tagline

AI-native operations for founders turning high-value opportunities into shipped
products, evidence packs, and completed submissions.

## What We Build

ContestOps AI ingests startup competitions, grants, accelerators, hackathons,
and RFPs, then converts them into executable application workspaces. It reads
rules, scores fit, creates checklists and evidence ledgers, drafts pitch and
form answers, prepares deck/video outlines, and runs external form automation
with receipts.

## Why Now

Founders are using AI to build faster, but the surrounding operational work is
still manual. Every serious opportunity demands proof: eligibility, product
evidence, traction, demos, videos, forms, and follow-up. ContestOps AI makes
that repeatable.

## AI Differentiation

AI is the operating layer: extraction, scoring, answer generation, critique,
evidence-gap detection, and automation planning. WAIBAv then executes the
prepared workflow through browser/form sessions and captures receipts.

## Traction

The current MVP has generated reusable opportunity packages, including Build
with Gemini XPRIZE and this RAISE packet. The next proof gate is five founder
conversations, two written validations, and one pilot or paid sprint receipt.

## RAISE Benefit

RAISE provides the right audience for this product: founders, AI operators,
investors, and partners who understand the pain of turning AI speed into real
startup execution. The competition would help validate the product, open pilot
channels, and accelerate the path to a repeatable AI application-ops platform.
