## Official RAISE Page

URL: https://www.raisesummit.com/startup-competition

Facts used:

- Applications are open for 2026.
- Event is July 8 and July 9, 2026 at Le Carrousel du Louvre, Paris.
- Prize/exposure pool is listed as EUR 10M+.
- Eligibility includes AI at the core of the product, total funding under EUR
  10M, at least two team members or dedicated cofounders, registered legal
  entity, and pre-seed/seed/Series A stage.
- Milestone section says applications close June 10, Top 100 on June 17, Top 10
  finalist selection on June 24, finalist pitch July 8, Top 3 main stage pitch
  July 9.
- FAQ says startups from any region can apply if at least one founder can
  attend finals in Paris.
- FAQ says jury looks at team strength, product distinctiveness, traction or
  validation, and opportunity scale.

Conflict noted:

- A lower call-to-action on the same page says "Application closes 1st of July."
  This package treats June 10 as the hard deadline because it appears in the
  official milestones and VC4A listing.
